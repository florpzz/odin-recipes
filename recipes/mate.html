Let's make a good argentinian mate

Description

Mate is a South American tea-like beverage. It's drunk all over Argentina, and it can be made in differnt forms. Let's get the basics on

What you need

  • A thermos
  • A gourd (it can be made from wood, glass, ceramic, among others)
  • A bombilla (metal straw)

Ingredients

  • Yerba mate
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put 1 liter of water to boil, and wait until it's 80Â°C
  2. Add the water to the thermos
  3. Take the gourd and fill it with yerba mate
  4. Put the gourd a little sideways until a little mountain is formed on the side
  5. Fill the sunken side with cold water
  6. Add the bombilla where you poured the cold water
  7. Take the thermos, and add the hot water where the bombilla is, take a sip, and then spit the infusion into a sink. This is done so the yerba mate isn't burnt when we add the hot water

Enjoy your mate!

Sidenote: You can add any herb you want to the infusion, or even coffee. Aditionally, you take it with sugar, but know I'll judge you for that.
